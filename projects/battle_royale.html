Battle Royale Fantasy

Graphics

A fun battle royale game where up to four player battle each other to the death in multiple maps with different weapons. All the input is done with the joysticks of the controller.

In Battle Royale Fantasy, players must face each other in a series rounds in different maps and with different weapons until one of them emerges victorious. The game was developed algon with fellow colleague Carmen Martín Moreno.

What I did

  • Core gameloop.
  • Character movement and weapons.
  • Level design.
  • Audio system.

Features

  • Local multiplayer with up to 4 players.
  • Battles in three maps and with three different weapon choices.
  • Full game cycle where the players battle in rounds until one of them wins 5 rounds.
  • Character customization. You can choose between different characters.
  • Full animations and vfx, including winning and losing animations.
  • Main and game over menus with character animations to make them more immersive.
  • Innovative controls where only the joysticks are needed. Each weapon is controlled differently with the right joystick.
